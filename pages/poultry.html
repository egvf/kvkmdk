Poultry

The poultry unit is established in kvk farm in the year of 2019 with the assistance of ICAR. The dimension of this unit is 30 X 20 feet in east west direction. The main objective of this unit is to raise the income by production of country birds for meat purpose as a part of integrated framing system. The shed capacity is 350 to 400 birds.
